# Demo Script

## 90-second flow

1. Open `개요` and state the problem: public ODA data is open but hard to convert into country-sector project plans.
2. Open `순위` and show Top 50 opportunity ranking.
3. Open `AI Builder`.
4. Select `CSO 탄자니아 공공행정`.
5. Click `RAG형 AI 사업기획서 생성`.
6. Show `근거 Citation`: CPS PDF, KOICA, WDI, and policy evidence IDs are visible.
7. Show `점수 기여도`: the recommendation is explainable, not a black box.
8. Download `Evidence Pack` and `1-page Brief`.
9. Open `AI검증`: RAG corpus, sensitivity, and hallucination controls.
10. Open `배포`: show Streamlit/GitHub QR.

## One-line closing

K-ODA Compass turns diplomatic CPS policy documents and development-cooperation public data into an evidence-grounded AI planning workflow that policy teams, CSOs, local governments, and companies can inspect, reuse, and validate.
